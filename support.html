Face Data and User Photos

Cutify allows users to upload, capture, or select photos that may contain faces in order to use AI hairstyle previews, face analysis, and hairstyle recommendation features.


What We Collect

We may process photos that contain a user's face or visible facial features when the user voluntarily uploads or captures a photo inside the app. We may also derive face-related attributes such as face shape, facial proportions, symmetry metrics, and landmark points solely to provide hairstyle analysis and visual preview features.

We do not collect or store biometric identifiers such as facial recognition data, face templates, or Face ID information. We do not use face data for identification, advertising, or tracking of any kind.


How We Use Face Data

Face-related image data is used only to provide the app's core functionality, including:

  • AI hairstyle generation
  • Hairstyle preview editing
  • Face shape analysis
  • Hairstyle recommendations based on visible facial structure

We do not use face-related data for identity verification, advertising, or user tracking.


Sharing

We use third-party AI services (such as Google Gemini) solely to process user images and generate hairstyle previews. These services receive images only for the duration of processing and do not use the data for identification, tracking, or advertising purposes. Photos are not sold or shared with any other third parties.


Retention

Face data and user photos are used solely to generate AI hairstyle previews and are not stored permanently. Uploaded photos are deleted automatically within 24 hours of upload — typically much sooner, as deletion occurs immediately after processing is complete. No face data is retained on our servers beyond this 24-hour window under any circumstances. Generated results may be temporarily stored on the user's device and can be deleted by the user at any time.


Third-Party Retention

When face data is processed by third-party AI services (such as Google Gemini), those services may temporarily store the image data solely to complete the generation request. This temporary storage does not exceed 24 hours and is deleted by the third-party service after processing is complete. These third-party services do not use face data for identification, advertising, tracking, or any purpose other than generating the requested hairstyle preview.


Analytics and Payment Services

Firebase Analytics and RevenueCat are used for app analytics and payment processing respectively. Neither Firebase Analytics nor RevenueCat collect, process, or store face data or user photos in any form.


User Control

Users may delete locally stored app content by clearing app data or uninstalling the app, where applicable.

Tokens and In-App Purchases

Cutify features consumable tokens that can be used to unlock hairstyle previews, AI generations, and hairstyle recommendations. Users can receive tokens for free via promo codes. Additional tokens are available for purchase in the following packs: 150 tokens, 500 tokens, and 1500 tokens. Token purchases are clearly marked as requiring a separate in-app purchase and are not included in the free download.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party AI services (such as Google Gemini) solely to process user images and generate hairstyle previews. These services receive images only for the duration of processing and do not use the data for identification, tracking, or advertising purposes.

We also use payment processors for in-app purchases and analytics tools to understand app usage. These services are required to handle data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable data protection laws.
